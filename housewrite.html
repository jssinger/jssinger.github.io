About Us

The TAG Quizbowl Team was founded in 2017 by Ketan Pamurthy, then a freshman at TAG. Ketan had previously played quizbowl in middle school, where he led his team to a top 50 finish at the 2017 MSNCT during 8th grade. TAG cemented its status as a top team in the Dallas quizbowl circuit in the 2019-2020 season, built around a core of Ketan, Jisoo, Jaik, and Andrew on an A team coached by Dr. Grant Mindle.

Below are some of the accomplishments of TAG quizbowl team.

  • 1st place at the Tal Atkins Memorial Quiz Bowl Tournament on September 7, 2019
  • 1st place at the TQBA Comet Challange on February 1, 2020
  • 1st place at the St. Marks School of Texas Invitational, Varsity Division, on Febraury 29, 2020
  • 1st place at the WAIT Online Knowledge Event on March 28, 2020
  • 1st place at the BHSAT Online 2020 Tournament on April 5, 2020
  • 1st place at the James Blair Bowl Online on April 25, 2020
